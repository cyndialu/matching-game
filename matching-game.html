Matching Game

Can you find the extra smiley face?

SCORE

Instruction:

When the game starts, there will be three smiley faces shown on the left side and two on the right.
The left and right sides are identical except for the left side having one extra face.
Each time you click on the correct face, you will be moved onto the next level with one more face than before.
If you are stuck, click on the hint button for help. You will be given 5 chances of hint and each time you click on the button, you will lose one chance.

Are you ready for the game?
Hit the start button and have fun!
